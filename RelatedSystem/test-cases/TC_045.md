## Test Case 45: CC Tool — call reservation reminder push (1 hour before)

**Test Case ID:** CC-CALL-REMINDER-PUSH-001  
**Test Case Title:** Verify call reservation reminder push (1 hour before) from CC Tool with app not launched and notifications ON  
**Priority:** High  
**Automation ID:** C00096  
**Grade:** D

**Role:** Tester (CC Reservation Tool access + Tsukasapo test device).

**Preconditions:**

- **User:** Tsukasapo user; **provisioning completed** for the selected environment.  
- **App:** **Not launched** from reservation through **observation of the reminder** in step 4 (unless a step explicitly says to open the app).  
- **Settings:** **Soluto app notification: ON**; **OS push notification: ON**.  
- **Environment:** Selected **per requirement** (SQA, UAT, etc.); tool and app build aligned to that line.  
- **CC Reservation Tool** URL recorded from [UAT_60. Access Information](https://asurion.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/ANSFK/pages/123336319/UAT_60.+Access+Information).

**Test Steps:**

| Step | Action | Expected Result |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | On the **test device**, ensure the **Tsukasapo app is not launched**. Confirm **Soluto app notification** is **ON** and **OS push notification** is **ON** for Soluto/Tsukasapo. | App remains **not launched**; both notification settings are **ON**. |
| 2 | Open **CC Reservation Tool**, sign in, and **create a call reservation** for the test user for a **future** date/time that will trigger a **reminder 1 hour before** the call (slot per tool / BA policy). **Do not** launch the Tsukasapo app after this step. | Reservation is **accepted** in CC Tool (success message, saved state, or equivalent). |
| 3 | In **CC Reservation Tool**, **confirm** the reservation is **visible** (list, detail, or saved state) for the test user/MDN. Keep Tsukasapo **not launched** through step 4 unless **Note** requires a separate **Mypage** check. | Reservation **appears** in CC Tool for that user (booking **active** or equivalent). |
| 4 | **Do not launch** the app before the reminder window. When **the reminder time is due** (**1 hour before** the reserved call time), observe **OS push** and **Soluto push** on the device. | **Push notification** is received **when the time has come for reminding** (**1 hour before** the reservation time), with **Title:** `【お電話のご予約時間が近づいております】`. |
