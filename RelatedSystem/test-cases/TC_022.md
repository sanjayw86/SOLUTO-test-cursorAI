## Test Case 22: Duplicate SMS — auto-stop, deliveryEnabled false, Teams/email/OpenSearch, recovery

**Test Case ID:** SMS-DUPLICATE-AUTOSTOP-001  
**Test Case Title:** Verify duplicate SMS triggers auto-stop, disables delivery (`deliveryEnabled` false), alerts (Teams Soluto-Alerts / email / OpenSearch), and recovery after Dev cleanup and re-enable  
**Priority:** High  
**Automation ID:** SMS_TEMP_001
**Grade:** D

**Preconditions:**

- **Environment:** **ISS SQA** + **Titan SQA** (URLs in **Test Data**).
- **SMS toggles:** **Welcome**, **Reminder1**, **Reminder2** **ON** per project.
- **Tools:** Postman, **ISS** and **Titan** DB access, **OpenSearch**, **Teams** (**Soluto-Alerts**), ops **email**.
- **Baseline:** **`deliveryEnabled`: true** (**GET** `smsDeliveryWorker`). For CSV variant, **`isNormalOperationMode`: true** if bulk requires normal mode.
- **Risk:** Test may require **dropping an index** — **Dev/LT** only, written approval, **rollback** plan, **SQA** only.
- **Non‑negotiable:** After duplicate is accepted, **SMS job must stop within 1 minute** (record time).

| 22 | Final **DB** sanity check. | Consistent with success. |
